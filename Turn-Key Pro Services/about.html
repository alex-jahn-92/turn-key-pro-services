About Turn Key Pro Services

Our Story

At Turn Key Pro Services, we didn't start this company just to fix homes—we started it to fix a problem.

A few years ago, our founder, noticed something troubling in our community. Hardworking homeowners were struggling to keep up with the rising costs of repairs and maintenance. Many were forced to choose between necessary fixes and other essentials—like groceries, medical bills, or their kids' education. Others fell victim to unreliable contractors who left them with half-finished work and empty wallets.

That's when we decided to do things differently.

We built Turn Key Pro Services on a simple belief: everyone deserves a safe, well-maintained home without breaking the bank. Our team isn't just made up of skilled technicians—we're neighbors, parents, and friends who genuinely care about the people we serve. We've been in your shoes, and we know how stressful home repairs can be. That's why we offer honest pricing, transparent service, and a commitment to doing the job right the first time.

Whether it's an emergency repair in the middle of the night or routine maintenance to prevent costly damage, we're here to take the stress off your shoulders. Because at the end of the day, our greatest reward isn't just fixing houses—it's helping people keep their homes, with dignity and peace of mind.

That's the Turn Key Pro difference. Your home matters to us—because you matter to us.
